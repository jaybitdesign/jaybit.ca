About Me

 My passion for technology started in early Junior High where I excelled in Electronic Technologies and continued into High School through Programming, Robotics and Design courses. I am fluent in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Java, mySQL, Ruby on Rails, JSON, Groovy and Python and also have extensive experience with e-commerce platforms such as WordPress, Shopify, Magento and Presta Shop. I like to spend my free time learning programming frameworks such as Twitter’s Bootstrap, Google’s Material Design, React.js, and jQuery. My passion is creating beautiful responsive websites, but I also consider myself a Full Stack developer.
